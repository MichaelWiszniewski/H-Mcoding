Rules of the MindDigits Challenge!

In the "MindDigits Challenge," you're given control over the rules of an entertaining game. Choose how many tries you'd want to make after first indicating the range of integers the mystery number will be within. Guessing the secret integer inside the selected range is your goal. The game gives you feedback after every guess as to whether you've found the mystery number or whether you still need to keep trying. The game continues until you predict the right number correctly or use up all of your permitted attempts. In the event that you exhaust your attempts, the game reveals the mystery number. You must use strategy and intuition to solve this fascinating number problem!

How do the difficulty ratings work?

Difficulty rating is defined by the size of the range of possible integers divided by the amount of alloted attemps. The higher the difficulty rating, the harder it is to guess the number! Therefore to increase the difficulty, you can either increase the range, or decrease the amount of attempts. To decrease the difficulty, you can either decrease the range, or increase the amount of attempts.
